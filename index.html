About

Alan's bold journey from the beginning.

  • 1952

    Humble Beginnings

    Born in an actual hospital with indoor plumbing, Alan would soon grown into a humble young man. A young man who occasionally snuck home for school for various Hostes products. A young man who "borrowed" his dad's car, and excelled at odometer maintenance.

  • 1970's

    He Still Had Hair

    Heading to exotic locales like Moscow Idaho, Alan explored the west. Running to bars and from Lumberjacks, Alan exceled at running in circles very fast. He would go "circle it up" in places like Walla Walla and Boise. And, he never, ever, smoked weed.

  • 1980's

    The Dark Ages

    Nobody can be sure what happend prior to the arrival of his children. What little do know is not suitable for most audiences. For our own sanity and safety, we have blocked most of these stories from memory, and choose to never think of them again. To sum it up, Alan did work stuff good here.

  • August 2017

    Phase Two - Retirement

    After a long career pushing various buttons and cursing at computer monitors, Alan is finally retiring. While he will no longer get discounted Save-A-Lot "merch" he can still get the five-finger discount anytime he chooses. I have no doubt that in the proud tradition of retired people, he will hold long conversations with casheers and always pay in cash, using exact change.
